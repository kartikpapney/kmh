MESS FACILITY
KMH has one of the best group of mess menials among the hostels at Tezpur University. New dishes are tried out on a regular basis so that the best food is available for the students. Mess managers are changed every month and this provides a chance to new members to implement their innovative ideas. Special dinner is provided on the last Sunday of every month.

CAFETERRIA
The hostel has a cafeteria located in the ground floor. It is open from 9:00am. to night 9:30 pm. This is a wonderful place for boarders to have a light-hearted chat after the busy schedule of the class hours.

WI-FI
The hostel is WI-Fi enabled so that students enabling boarders to access to the internet even within their own rooms. The accessing speed is very good for downloading files.

COMMON ROOM
The Common Room is the most happening place in the hostel. Kanchenjungha Men’s Hostel comes alive every evening as boarders gather around the television to catch a glimpse of their favorite programs. There is a good music system along with carrom and table tennis boards in the Common Room. It has 300 chairs for the boarders of the hostel which is used during general body meetings and post-dinner talks which are organized on a regular basis.

SPORTS
In it’s very first year of inception, Kanchenjungha Men’s Hostel has left an indelible impression in the sporting arena of Tezpur University. The year began with the intake of a large number of freshers, who have turned out to be talented, hardworking and promising. We, the boarders of the hostel could sense the beginning of a new era in the sports scene at TU, and as the year proceeded, it did turn out to be so. KMH continued to rock the sports scene this year as it maintained its overall 1st position in sports at TU. There were plenty of moments to savor along with a few disappointments but the overall enthusiasm was unmatched. It all began with a superb victory in outdoor sports, in which we dominated both the track and field events. We participated in the March Past event this year. Students got many medals in running, chess, body building etc. With the kind of talent that we have, we are sure that the number of medals will only grow next year.

MINI-GARDEN
Hostel is currently going through a makeover to enhance its beauty. We intend to do so by making a flower nursery in front of and all the corners of the hostel.

COMPUTER ROOM
Although you may find a PC in most of the hostel rooms, you will always find guys busy in the compy room at any time of the day or night. The computer room provides computing facilities for all inmates for 24 hours. 3computers along with comfortable chairs ensures that the centre is hardly ever unoccupied. It caters to the service of all those hostelites who cannot afford personal computers.

NEWSPAPER
National and Regional Newpapers are provided on a regular basis.
WASHING ROOM
We have a washing machine for the hostelites to save their important time.
