Welcome


  Kanchenjungha Men’s Hostel boasts of excellent infrastructure with a Computer Centre, fully equipped with Sports Kits and readily available to the boarders. Kanchanjungha Men's Hostel is presently one of the largest men's hostel of Tezpur University having the strength to accommodate more than 400 boarders.

It is a newly constructed RCC- 3 storied hostel with all modern facilities. The hostel has been operating since 31st August, 2009. Kanchanjungha Men’s Hostel was inaugurated on 31st August 2009 by Sri Paban Singh Ghatowar, Hon’ble Member of Parliament. Kanchenjungha Men’s Hostel has scaled new heights of excellence in diverse fields of activities with its highly enthusiastic and talented inmates.
