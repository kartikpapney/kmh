    MESS FACILITY

  • KMH has one of the best group of mess menials among the hostels at Tezpur University. New dishes are tried out on a regular basis so that the best food is available for the students. Mess managers are changed every month and this provides a chance to new members to implement their innovative ideas. Special dinner is provided on the last Sunday of every month.
    Statement of Expenditure of Mess Click here.


    LIBRARY

  • The hostel has a small library covering a small range of books. Hostel boarders can issue these books when they require them. The range of books varies from the religious books of various religion, poetry, novels and books for competitive examination.


    WI FI

  • The hostel is WI-Fi enabled so that students enabling boarders to access to the internet even within their own rooms. The accessing speed is very good for downloading files.


    COMMON ROOM

  • The Common Room is the most happening place in the hostel. Kanchenjungha Men’s Hostel comes alive every evening as boarders gather around the television to catch a glimpse of their favorite programs. There is a good music system along with carrom and table tennis boards in the Common Room. It has 300 chairs for the boarders of the hostel which is used during general body meetings and post-dinner talks which are organized on a regular basis.


    GUEST ROOM

  • The hostel has guest room facilities which can be availed on payment basis. The hostel has six double bed guest rooms and two single bed rooms. Bathroom facilities are also provided which are attached to respective rooms.


    COMPUTER ROOM

  • The hostel has a computer room which provides computing facilities for all inmates for 24 hours. There are three computers along with comfortable chairs ensures that the centre is hardly ever unoccupied. It caters to the service of all those hostelites who cannot afford personal computers.


    WASHING ROOM

  • There is a special arrangement of a washing machine in the hostel. Washing rates are economical and adds to one of the most needed facilities of the hostel with the sole concern of the welfare of the boarders.


    UTILITY SHOP

  • There is a utility shop in the ground floor of the hostel with all the facilities essential for the boarders of the hostel. The shop provides 24 hours of service during the examination period to facilitate the needs.


    SPORTS

  • There are sufficient sports facilities and equipments in the hostel for the boarders of the hostel. Games like badminton, volleyball, carom, chess, table tennis, etc can be played without any disadvantages or limitations within the hostel premises. The hostel provides suitable free space for the outdoor games and it also consists of a suitable spacious room to carry out different indoor game activities and other extra curricular activities.


    MINI GARDEN

  •  In front of the hostel there is a mini garden with varieties of seasonal and non seasonal flowers. Regular care and watering and fertilizations are carried out in the garden so that the beauty and cleanliness of the environment of the hostel is maintained.


    NEWSPAPER

  • National and Regional Newpapers are provided on a regular basis.


    PARKING FACILITY

  • The hostel has parking facilities to provide parking space for the vehicles of the respective boarders. There are two parking areas within the hostel premise for spacious parking facilities.
